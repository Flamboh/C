Cadmium (Cd) is a resource. It can be found on planets orbiting Class K/M systems (red stars).

Game Description
A chromatic metal, generated by fusion in the heart of a star. Such stellar material ends up forming deposits in the crust of local planets.

Cadmium is found on planets orbiting red stars, and can be placed in a Refiner to create purified Chromatic Metal for use in the manufacture of advanced technologies.

Source
In resource deposits on planets orbiting red stars. █████ ███ █
Dismantling Pulse Engine Upgrade modules.
